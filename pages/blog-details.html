Discover this beautiful village .

John Doe  |  27.07.2020 10:10  |  15 comments


The beauty of my village is ineffable. It is situated on the top of a mountain. It has just a few houses situated remotely from one another.

It overlooks a beautiful dell through which a silver rivulet flows like a silver thread. On the either side of the rivulet are terraced fields in which grow flowers and plants of attractive and bright hues.

The climate surrounding the village is always pleasant. It is a perfect paradise of beautiful things and quietness. There is no din of city life here. The sky is always pure azure; and the clouds are as white and appear as if huge pure white cotton bales are flitting merrily.

Beyond the valley are the undulating mountains covered with pristine forests.
